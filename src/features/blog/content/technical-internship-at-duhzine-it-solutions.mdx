I recently completed an intensive three-month technical internship at **Duhzine IT Solutions Pvt Ltd** (Nov 2025 - Feb 2026), and it was a transformative experience for my growth as a developer.

## Core Focus Areas

During my time at Duhzine, I had the opportunity to dive deep into two major disciplines:

### 1. Fullstack Development
I contributed to building and adapting emerging technologies. This involved working with modern frameworks and ensuring high productivity across both frontend and backend systems. I focused on:
- Writing clean, maintainable, and type-safe code with **TypeScript**.
- Building responsive interfaces with **React** and **Tailwind CSS**.
- Fostering strong collaboration with team members to solve complex technical challenges.

### 2. UX/UI Design
Beyond coding, I applied design principles to enhance user interfaces and experiences. Working as a UX/UI Designer intern allowed me to:
- Create intuitive user flows.
- Design polished interfaces that balance aesthetics with functionality.
- Develop a better eye for the "small details" that truly matter in professional software.

## Key Learnings & Skills

This internship wasn't just about the technology; it was about the professional standards of the industry. I honed several key skills:
- **Analytical Skills**: Tackling real-world problems with data-driven solutions.
- **Team Collaboration**: Participating in agile workflows and cross-functional team meetings.
- **Project Discipline**: Delivering high-quality work within tight deadlines.
